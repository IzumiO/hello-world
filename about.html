How It Works!

REGISTER FOR AN ACCOUNT

The first thing you need to do is register for an account.
After registering you will be able to submit requests for pick up and have your food delivered to your door!

ORDER YOUR FOOD

Place an order for food from a local restaurant and the sytem will automatically find the nearest driver at the best cost.

CONFIRM YOUR REQUESTS

Before any request is submitted, you have the chance to review it and make any changes or cancellations.
